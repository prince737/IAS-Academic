Admission Process

When we admit a student to IAS, it's as if we're choosing a team to climb a very interesting, fairly rugged mountainâ€”together.

We obviously want student who have the training, stamina and passion for the climb.

At the same time, we want each to add something useful or intriguing to the team, from a wonderful temperament or sense of humour to compelling personal experiences, to a wide range of individual gifts, talents, interests and achievements.

We are emphatically not looking for a batch of identical perfect climbers; we are looking for a richly varied team of capable people who will support, surprise and inspire each other.

Preparing yourself for your future, have to follow two things:

  • Making sure you're ready to do the hard work, and
  • Taking the time to really explore things that interest you, both inside and outside of the Institution.
